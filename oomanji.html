When Ooredoo Meets Jumanji — Oomanji is created. Oomanji is a 7 step journey to find treasure across the map of your country.


In Myanmar, the user has to travel the entire country and find 7 gems located across the various location, and at each level, he wins prizes. Behind this gamification runs a complex algorithm that is able to reward users basis their segment, AoN, and ARPU thereby maximizing returns per user and also giving them the excitement of winning something. The game is based on human physiology of “Let me play again, maybe I am lucky this time”.


Oomanji can be customized to your country with your country map. The gems can be changed to anything that is specific to your country and the user can be asked to collect those gems or items. You can also increase the 7 steps to 10 steps or decide to decrease it to 5 steps. It's all your call. The music can be customized and the parrot can be changed to a national bird of your country or any other preferred animal The creatives are already ready. TVC is also available for you to customize and the music can be recorded in your language.
